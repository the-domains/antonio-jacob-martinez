Near the Egress with Antonio Martinez | Grand Center - The Intersection of Art and Life

Near the Egress began as a "what if" experiment in which Martinez's interest in modern dryplate tintypes at that time in 2005 was later inspired by John Aasp's lecture on the properties of a moving still image and influenced by Stan Brakhage's experimental films. Near the Egress is a three part series comprised of the original modern dryplate tintype, the stop-animation video, and digital images that combine the details of the black and white negative and the ephemeral effects produced by the tintype.
